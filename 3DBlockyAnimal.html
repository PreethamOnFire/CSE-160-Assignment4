Please use browser that supports "canvas"

Notes for graders:
The animation button is a toggle. Clicking the button will stop and start the animation.
Clicking and dragging the canvas will rotate the figure about the y-axis.
Shift-Clicking the canvas will cause a flying animation that lasts for about 11 seconds.
I implemented cone primitives and there are 4 of them on the wings.
The zoom slider mainly zooms out.
The flapping and tailwagging sliders allow you to control the full animation. The inidividual joint sliders allow you to control specific joints within that part.
